Adidas Company

Adidas German pronunciation: is a German athletic apparel and footwear corporation headquartered in Herzogenaurach, Bavaria, Germany. It is the largest sportswear manufacturer in Europe, and the second largest in the world, after Nike.

Adidas History

The company was founded by Adolf "Adi" Dassler who made sports shoes in his mother's scullery or laundry room in Herzogenaurach, Germany after his return from World War I.

Adidas logo

Meaning of the Three Stripes: There is no definitive meaning assigned to the three stripes by Adidas. Instead, they have been interpreted in various ways: Some see them as a representation of a mountain, symbolizing the challenges and goals athletes strive to overcome.
